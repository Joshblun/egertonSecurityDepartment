Our Partners

We collaborate with several organizations to enhance security at the university. Our key partners include:

  • National Police Service: We work closely with local law enforcement to ensure campus safety.
  • National Youth Service: This partnership helps in maintaining order during large student events and activities.
  • Kenya Wildlife Service: This collaboration ensures the protection of both students and wildlife around campus areas.
